Page Layout

    The layout is too plain and not appealing to the target audience.
    The site header/logo are consistent.
    The page title does include the company name.
    Page footer contains copyright and email address but not last update.
    There is little repetition in the layout from tab to tab. Alignment by the photos looks terrible. Contrast is acceptable.
